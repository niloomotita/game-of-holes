Fun with the bare hands
The Game of Holes
How to play?

Instructions

All needed is a group of 5 to 10 people. They need to have one fist and one finger each. Follow the instructions below.

  • The Master

    Choose one random person as the Master.

  • The Hole

    Everybody except the master forms a hole using one of their hands, left or right doesn't matter.

  • The Circle

    Put all the holes sticking together on a table.

  • The Fingers

    Everybody raise the index finger of their free hand into the air.

  • The COMMANDS

    Master has two commands: "Home" and "neighbor". Master calls the commands randomly and playfully.

    Before each command all the fingers should be in ready state, up in the air.

  • HOME Command

    When master says "Home", players put their fingers to their own holes. Master doesn't need to finger any hole.

  • neighbor command

    When master says "neighbor", players put their fingers to any other holes, except theirown. Master also puts his/her finger to one of the holes. As the master doesn't have a hole in the game, there's always one hole missing. It's like the game of chairs. The finger left out, loses and is called the hole-less. Also if there are two or more fingers in one hole, the finger(s) getting into the hole later, will be the hole-less.

  • The hole-less

    At each round the finger left out without a hole, is the hole-less. Make that person do something funny. For example writing "constantinopole" with their butt on the wall?
